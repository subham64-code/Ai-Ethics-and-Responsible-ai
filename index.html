Three Superpowers

What Can AI Do?

AI has three main abilities – just like our brain, eyes, and voice!

Data Science

🧮 Find Patterns

AI looks at numbers and words to guess what will happen next.

e.g. Predict weather, sort emails

Computer Vision

📷 See the World

AI can recognise faces, objects, and even read handwriting!

e.g. Face unlock, medical scans

NLP

🗣️ Understand Words

AI reads, listens, and talks back to us in our own language.

e.g. Chatbots, translation

Many smart devices use all three together!

Module 3

Bias & Fairness in AI

AI learns from data. If data is incomplete or unfair, AI choices will be unfair too!

🚨 UNFAIR EXAMPLE:

An AI job recruitment system only picks or displays opportunities to boys.

👦✅ 👧❌

NO! ❌ Everyone deserves equal chances!

🛠️ HOW TO FIX BIAS?

  • 🍏 Use diverse data from everyone.
  • 🧪 Test systems carefully before launch.
  • 👀 Always include human review checks.

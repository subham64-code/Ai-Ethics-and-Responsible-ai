# 🤖 AI Ethics & Responsible AI - Interactive Presentation

An engaging, interactive presentation about AI Ethics and Responsible AI designed for students in Class 3 to 8.

## ✨ Features

- **Interactive Slides**: Navigate through 5 comprehensive slides about AI ethics
- **Bilingual Support**: Available in English and Odia (ଓଡ଼ିଆ)
- **Animations & Effects**: Beautiful floating animations, glow effects, and interactive elements
- **Quiz Section**: Interactive quiz cards with reveal animations
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Educational Content**: Covers Privacy, Bias, Misinformation, and Accountability

## 🚀 Live Demo

Visit the live presentation: [AI Ethics Presentation](https://your-vercel-url.vercel.app)

## 📚 Topics Covered

1. **What is AI Ethics?** - Introduction to ethical AI principles
2. **Privacy & Bias** - Understanding data privacy and AI bias
3. **Misinformation & Accountability** - Combating fake news and responsibility
4. **Interactive Quiz** - Test your knowledge with fun questions

## 🎯 Educational Objectives

- Understand the importance of AI ethics
- Learn about data privacy and protection
- Recognize bias in AI systems
- Identify misinformation and fake news
- Understand accountability in AI development

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **HTML5** - Structure and content
- **CSS3** - Advanced animations and styling
- **Vanilla JavaScript** - Interactive functionality
- **Canvas API** - Particle background effects

## 📱 Usage

1. Open `hello.html` in any modern web browser
2. Use arrow keys or navigation buttons to move between slides
3. Click on interactive elements to see animations
4. Switch between English and Odia using the language toggle
5. Take the quiz by clicking on question cards

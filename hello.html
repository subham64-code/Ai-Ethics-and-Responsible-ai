What is AI Ethics?

💬  "Ethics asks: What SHOULD technology do — not just what CAN it do?"
⚖️
FAIR
Treats every person equally, no exceptions!
🛡️
SAFE
Protects people from harm & danger
🔍
TRANSPARENT
Honest & open about how it works
🤝
HELPFUL
Benefits everyone in society — you too!
🎯 THINK & SHARE: If a robot delivers food faster to rich areas but ignores poor areas — is it ethical? Why?

⚖️ Bias in AI

Bias = Unfair treatment because AI learned from bad or one-sided data.

⚖️
Fair AI treats everyone equally!
📊  Unfair Data Goes In
e.g. Only photos from one region
⬇️
🤖  AI Learns Wrong Patterns
Doesn't recognise diverse faces
⬇️
✅ The Fix: Use diverse, fair data + Human review!
